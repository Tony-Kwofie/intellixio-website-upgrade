MEET OUR FOUNDER

Anthony Eddei Kwofie

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Anthony Eddei Kwofie is the Founder & Chief Executive Officer of Intellixio Technologies Ltd. He established the company with a vision of helping organisations embrace intelligent technology that simplifies operations, improves decision-making and drives sustainable growth.

He holds a BSc in Business Administration from Central University, Accra, Ghana, and an MSc in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from the University of Hull, London Campus, United Kingdom.

By combining business knowledge with expertise in artificial intelligence, Anthony is leading the development of a growing ecosystem of intelligent business platforms that empower organisations to innovate, operate more efficiently and prepare confidently for the future.
