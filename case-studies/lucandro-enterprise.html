CLIENT OVERVIEW

About Lucandro Enterprise

Lucandro Enterprise is a Ghanaian industrial supplies company providing high-quality products and engineering solutions to businesses across the mining, oil & gas, construction and manufacturing sectors.

As the business continued to grow, managing inventory, customers, suppliers and sales across multiple operational locations became increasingly complex. The company needed a modern platform capable of delivering real-time visibility, operational efficiency and long-term scalability.

THE CHALLENGE

Business Challenges

Lucandro Enterprise required a centralised solution that could simplify operations, improve visibility and support future business growth.

Inventory Control

Improve inventory visibility and maintain accurate stock records across business operations.

Sales Management

Streamline sales processing, credit sales and customer payment tracking.

VAT Compliance

Automate VAT calculations and improve financial reporting accuracy.

Multi-Location Operations

Manage inventory and business activities across multiple operational locations.

Business Intelligence

Gain real-time reporting and operational insights for better decision-making.

Operational Efficiency

Replace fragmented processes with one integrated cloud-based operations platform.

THE SOLUTION

A Unified Platform For Smarter Operations

Intellixio Technologies implemented Intellixio OPS to help Lucandro Enterprise centralise inventory, sales, procurement and operational reporting into one secure cloud-based platform.

Instead of relying on disconnected processes, management now has a single source of truth for inventory, customers, suppliers, sales and business performance.

What Was Implemented

Intellixio OPS was configured with the modules Lucandro Enterprise needs to efficiently manage daily operations and support future growth.

Inventory Management

Products, stock levels, serial tracking and inventory movements.

Sales & POS

Point of Sale, sales processing, credit sales and payments.

Customer Management

Customer records, statements and purchase history.

Supplier Management

Supplier profiles, purchases and performance reporting.

Warehouse Management

Multiple locations, stock transfers and inventory control.

VAT Management

Automated VAT calculations and tax reporting.

Reports & Analytics

Sales, inventory, profit, VAT and performance reporting.
