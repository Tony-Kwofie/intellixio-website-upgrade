CLIENT OVERVIEW

Helping a Growing Industrial Supplies Company Modernise Operations

Lucandro Enterprise is a Ghanaian industrial supplies company serving organisations across the mining, oil & gas, construction and manufacturing sectors. As the business continued to grow, management recognised the need for a modern business platform capable of centralising operations, improving inventory visibility and providing accurate real-time information across the organisation.

Working closely with Intellixio Technologies, Lucandro Enterprise implemented Intellixio OPS to replace fragmented processes with one intelligent cloud-based platform that now supports inventory management, sales, customer and supplier management, VAT compliance, reporting and executive decision-making.

THE CHALLENGE

Supporting Growth Through Digital Transformation

THE SOLUTION

One Connected Platform. Complete Operational Visibility.

Following a detailed assessment of Lucandro Enterprise's operational requirements, Intellixio Technologies deployed Intellixio OPS as the organisation's central business management platform.

The implementation brought together inventory management, sales, customer management, supplier management, VAT compliance, executive dashboards and business reporting within one secure cloud-based environment.

Instead of relying on disconnected systems, management now benefits from one integrated platform providing real-time operational visibility, improved collaboration and reliable business insights.
